EduBridge Initiative is a non-profit platform that aims to connect financially disadvantaged students with patrons who are willing to sponsor their educational journey.By bridging this gap we aim to reduce
inequality and promote academic success.

"If you educate one person,you can change a life.
If you educate many,you can change the world" - SHAI RESHEF


OUR VISION

Is to create a world where one's belonging education or future
is not determined by financial backgroud,rather every student can
have equal access to education.


OUR MISSION

To enable connection between financially disadvantaged students with
potential patrons who are willing to sponsor their educational journey.


OUR OBJECTIVES

1.Eliminate financial barriers to education.
2.Build meaningful sponsor relationships.
3.Empower future leaders.
4.Create long-term social impact.
